# Competitor Analysis Agent

The Competitor Analysis Agent helps you understand who you're competing against and how to position your offering. It identifies competitors, analyzes their strengths and weaknesses, and develops differentiation strategies.

---

## What It Does

Go into competitive situations prepared:

- **Identify competitors** — Who's likely bidding on this opportunity
- **Analyze capabilities** — What each competitor offers
- **Compare features** — Side-by-side capability matrix
- **Develop positioning** — How to differentiate yourself
- **Prepare counter-tactics** — Responses to competitor claims

---

## What You Get

**Competitor Profiles** — For each identified competitor:
- Company overview
- Relevant capabilities
- Typical positioning and messaging
- Known strengths in this space
- Known weaknesses or gaps

**Competitive Matrix** — Feature-by-feature comparison across:
- Technical capabilities
- Industry experience
- Geographic coverage
- Pricing approach
- Service offerings

**SWOT Analysis** — For your position vs. each competitor:
- Strengths you can leverage
- Weaknesses to address
- Opportunities to exploit
- Threats to mitigate

**Win Themes** — Key messages to emphasize:
- What makes you unique
- Why you're the best choice
- Proof points to include

**Counter-Positioning** — How to respond when competitors:
- Claim superiority in specific areas
- Raise concerns about your solution
- Highlight their unique features

## When to Use It

| Situation | Use Competitor Analysis? |
|---|---|
| Major competitive bid | Yes |
| Understanding the competitive landscape | Yes |
| Developing proposal win themes | Yes |
| Preparing for orals/presentations | Yes |
| Internal opportunity assessment | Use Deal Qualification first |
| Detailed RFP requirements analysis | Use Requirements Review |

---

## Input

The agent works from your project documents. For best results, include:

- The RFP or requirements document
- Any incumbent information mentioned
- Industry context if available

The more context about the opportunity, the better the competitor identification.

---

## Tips for Better Results

**Add context** — If you know specific competitors are likely, include that in your project notes

**Review and validate** — The agent identifies likely competitors, but your market knowledge may reveal others

**Update your approach** — Use the counter-positioning tactics in your proposal messaging

**Share with the team** — The competitive matrix is useful for proposal writers and presenters

# Proposal ScoreMaster Agent

The Proposal ScoreMaster Agent evaluates proposals across 16 weighted dimensions, providing a comprehensive scoring matrix that highlights strengths and improvement priorities.

---

## What It Does

Get an objective score for your proposal:

- **Score 16 dimensions** — Weighted evaluation across all critical areas
- **Calculate overall score** — Weighted total based on importance
- **Identify priorities** — Which dimensions need the most work
- **Benchmark quality** — Understand where you stand

---

## The 16 Scoring Dimensions

Each dimension has an assigned weight reflecting its typical importance in evaluations:

| Dimension | Weight | What It Measures |
|---|---|---|
| Technical Approach | 15% | Solution quality and methodology |
| Business Value | 12% | ROI and value proposition |
| Management Plan | 10% | Project governance and oversight |
| Staffing | 10% | Team qualifications and availability |
| Past Performance | 10% | Relevant experience and references |
| ROI | 10% | Return on investment for client |
| Cost | 8% | Pricing competitiveness and clarity |
| Innovation | 8% | Novel approaches and improvements |
| Risk Management | 7% | Risk identification and mitigation |
| Market Positioning | 6% | Industry credibility and presence |
| Value Proposition | 6% | Clear articulation of benefits |
| Compliance | 5% | Meeting mandatory requirements |
| Win Theme | 5% | Compelling differentiators |
| Security | 4% | Security approach and credentials |
| Competitive Advantage | 4% | Unique strengths vs. competitors |
| Business Continuity | 3% | Resilience and continuity planning |

---

## What You Get

**Scoring Matrix:**
- Raw score (1-10) for each dimension
- Weight applied
- Weighted score
- Comments explaining the score

**Overall Score:**
- Weighted total across all dimensions
- Rating category (Excellent/Good/Fair/Poor)

**Improvement Priorities:**
- Top 3 lowest-scoring dimensions highlighted
- Specific recommendations for each
- Expected impact of improvement

**Strength Summary:**
- Top 3 highest-scoring dimensions
- What's working well
- How to emphasize in the proposal

---

## Score Interpretation

| Score Range | Rating | What It Means |
|---|---|---|
| 8.0-10.0 | Excellent | Highly competitive, minor polish only |
| 6.5-7.9 | Good | Solid foundation, targeted improvements needed |
| 5.0-6.4 | Fair | Significant gaps, focused work required |
| Below 5.0 | Poor | Major issues, substantial revision needed |

---

## When to Use It

| Situation | Use Proposal ScoreMaster? |
|---|---|
| Want an objective quality assessment | Yes |
| Need to prioritize improvement efforts | Yes |
| Benchmarking proposal quality | Yes |
| Comparing multiple proposal versions | Yes |
| Need detailed red team review | Use Response Review instead |
| Need gap analysis vs. requirements | Use Requirements-Response Strategy |

---

## Input Requirements

Upload your proposal documents:
- Complete proposal response
- All major sections included

The agent works best with a substantially complete draft. Partial proposals may score lower on completeness dimensions.

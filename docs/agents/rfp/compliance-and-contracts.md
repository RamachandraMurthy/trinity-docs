# Compliance & Contracts Agents

> **Prerequisite:** These agents require a project with uploaded RFP documents. See [RFP Advisor Setup](/docs/rfp-advisor) for how to create a project and upload documents.

This page covers two related analysis capabilities: **Compliance & Certification** analysis and **Contract Terms Analysis**. Both help you understand regulatory and contractual risks in an RFP.

---

## Compliance & Certification Agent

Analyzes compliance requirements, regulatory obligations, and certification needs from the RFP.

### What It Does

- **Identify certifications** — What certifications are required (SOC 2, ISO 27001, etc.)
- **Map regulations** — Regulatory frameworks by jurisdiction
- **Assess gaps** — Where you meet requirements vs. where you have gaps
- **Plan remediation** — Implementation roadmap for compliance gaps

### The Pipeline (7 agents)

Document Analysis → Regulatory Requirements → Certification Standards → Geographic Compliance → Risk Assessment → Compliance Strategy → Review

### What You Get

**Certification Inventory:**
- Required certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001, FedRAMP, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, GDPR, etc.)
- Your current status for each
- Gap analysis

**Regulatory Framework:**
- Applicable regulations by jurisdiction
- Specific requirements from each
- Compliance obligations

**Risk Assessment:**
- Compliance gaps with risk ratings
- Mitigation strategies
- Implementation timeline

**Compliance Roadmap:**
- Prioritized actions
- Timeline for achieving compliance
- Resource requirements

---

## Contract Terms Analysis Agent

Analyzes contract terms, identifies risks, and develops negotiation strategies.

### What It Does

- **Extract terms** — Identify all significant contract terms
- **Assess risk** — Rate each term by risk level
- **Plan negotiation** — Develop strategies for contentious terms
- **Guide decisions** — Go/No-Go framework based on terms

### The Pipeline (6 agents)

Document Analysis → Contract Terms Extractor → Risk Assessment → Negotiation Strategy → Executive Report → Review

### What You Get

**Contract Terms Inventory:**
- All identified terms
- Category (liability, payment, IP, termination, etc.)
- Risk classification

**Risk Matrix:**
- Risk rating (High/Medium/Low)
- Impact type (Financial, Operational, Compliance, Legal)
- Probability assessment

**Negotiation Playbook:**
- Recommended position for each contentious term
- Fallback positions
- Walk-away points

**Go/No-Go Framework:**
- Terms that must be negotiated
- Terms that are acceptable
- Deal-breaker terms

---

## When to Use These Agents

| Situation | Which Agent? |
|---|---|
| Need to understand compliance requirements | Compliance & Certification |
| Want to assess certification gaps | Compliance & Certification |
| Need to analyze contract terms | Contract Terms Analysis |
| Preparing for contract negotiation | Contract Terms Analysis |
| Want a complete risk picture | Run both |
